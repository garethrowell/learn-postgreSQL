
Users, groups, roles, incoming connections at role level

users ~ users or applications 
groups ~ collections of users that usually share object permissions

role ~ can be users or groups of users

if exists ~ use in scripts for managing users and other db objects

deleting groups does #not# cascade to users

\du shows all available groups and users
\drg shows all groups that role is a member of...

connections

pg_hba.conf is the "firewall". It contains users and connection information
that is approved or blocked
